VR Horror - Adventure demo game

Platform: Oculus Rift (DK2)

The plot of the game takes place in an old and dark house. The main actor in the game has a flashlight so that he can explore the environment, as well as the smartphone that he uses to defend himself from the enemy. The main villain is a ghost that passes through all the rooms and attacks the main actor. The ghost can attack him at any moment so the actor must prepare in time for the defense. Remaster in 2021/22 with electroencephalogram (EEG) device for mind-reading and enhancements from AI models.

Vortex educational platform

In Vortex you can hold presentations and classes over the network in virtual reality. Participants will have opportunity to attend introduction session before they pay participation for course and give a feedback. Teachers will have the opportunity to share their knowledge with no cost.

The platform is supported by virtual agents, AI cloud services (text to speech), and blockchain technologies. Multiplatform development for PC, mobile, and game consoles. Papers: VoRtex Enterprise: Decentralized Virtual Reality Blockchain-based Platform; Review of Modern Virtual Reality HMD Devices and Development Tools.
